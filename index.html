Dalaguete CARES

Field-operations companion app for LGU Dalaguete

CARES is an internal field-validation mobile application for authorized personnel of the Local Government Unit of Dalaguete, Cebu, Philippines. It brings three LGU service modules into a single offline-capable app for field officers.

Modules

AMIA

Farmer Validation

Field validation of AMIA (Adaptation and Mitigation Initiative in Agriculture) farmer records — GPS-tagged farm visits, photos, and RSBSA record confirmation.

DFRB

Vehicles & Franchises

Dalaguete Franchise & Regulatory Board module — review and field-verify registered vehicles and transport franchises against eTracs records.

BPLS

Business Permits

Business Permit & Licensing System module — review and field-verify business permits, registered owners, and lines of business on site.
